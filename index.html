Welcome to Dublin!


The Setting

It's the late 1880's and Dublin is in a state of turmoil. Recent attacks on civilians supposedly by Jack the Ripper and large portions of the city burning down have left the citizens in shock. A small group of would-be-heroes band together to try and take back their city from the strange monsters that haunt it's streets.


Factions

  • The Rippers:

    Jack may have stolen their name and their numbers may be dwindling but this is the group that our heroes joined to keep Dublin safe.

  • The Cabal:

    The villainous society setup by Jack the Ripper to fight the Rippers, though hurt by the loss of their leader they are using Dublin as a new base of operations.

  • The Vampires:

    Long thought wiped out in Dublin a few old vampires appear to have survived by separating themselves from the Cababl.

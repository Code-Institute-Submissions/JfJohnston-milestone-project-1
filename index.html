Welcome to Dublin!


The Setting

It's the late 1880's and Dublin is in a state of turmoil. A mysterious someone rumoured to be Jack the Ripper has been attacking civilians, leaving the city's denizens in shock. A small group of would-be-heroes band together to try and take back their city from the strange monsters that haunt it's streets.
As tension mounts strange new drugs start to flood the streets and unknown to the citizens the fight for the soul of dublin is faught in the shadows. Will the Rippers, their name sullied by Jack, be able to hold back the darkness? Or will the night swallow them whole?


Factions

  • The Rippers:

    Jack may have stolen their name and their numbers may be dwindling but this is the group that our heroes joined to keep Dublin safe.

  • The Cabal:

    The villainous society setup by Jack the Ripper to fight the Rippers, though hurt by the loss of their leader they are using Dublin as a new base of operations.

  • The Vampires:

    Long thought wiped out in Dublin a few old vampires appear to have survived by separating themselves from the Cabal.

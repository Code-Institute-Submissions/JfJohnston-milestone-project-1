Introducing The Champions

Biddy

Biddy started life as a circus performer and acrobat. Until one faithful night the "wolfman" in the troupe transformed into a werewolf. Biddy was lucky to escape with her life and join the rippers in the hopes of making sure this would happen to no one else.

Garrett Fitzgerald

Born into wealth and galmour as the sire of the Fitzgerald family Garrett's life could easily have taken a different turn to one of indifference towards his fellow men. However almost to spite his father he took up the trade of a doctor and became a ripper.

Dorian Hayes

Hayes grew up in a remote villiage and himself and his brother had a habit of wandering. One day while wandering they encountered an old crone in the forest. James

James lived and worked in New Orleans for a long time. A born fighter James worked hard in underground rings to prove his strength and deal with his anger issues. During a bout some Rippers in America saw potential and recruited him, before mysteriously sending him to Ireland.

Kitty

The oldest among the group Kitty is a mysterious figure, sent to the nuns after being declared insane when her child died she later became a prostitue and lied cheated and stole to get her freedom. She talks little about her past and tries to hide her powers from the group, with varying success.

Lucian

Born a podigy with too much curiosity Lucian became a quick stand out student of medicine. He stood out enough that he caught the attention of a small faction of the Rippers, however his mentor unbeknownst to others had gone crazy and experimented on Lucian implanting him with parts of a demon that Lucian now lives with.
